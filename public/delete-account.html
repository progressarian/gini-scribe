Delete Your Account

For the Gini Health app (MyHealth Genie) on Google Play

This page explains how to request deletion of your Gini Health account and the personal data associated with it.

How to request account deletion

  1. Send an email to rsaini@ginihealth.in with the subject "Account Deletion Request".
  2. Include the name and phone number registered with your Gini Health account so we can locate it.
  3. We will verify your identity (we may contact you on your registered phone number) and process your request within 30 days.
  4. You will receive a confirmation once deletion is complete.
Email a Deletion Request

You can also ask your clinic's front desk to submit the request on your behalf.

What is deleted and what is kept

DataWhat happens
Account & login details (name, phone number, password) Deleted
Health data you logged in the app (vitals, symptoms, meals, goals, reminders) Deleted
Documents and photos you uploaded in the app Deleted
Device data (push-notification tokens, analytics identifiers) Deleted
Hospital / clinic medical records (prescriptions, lab results, visit records created by your care provider) Kept by the healthcare provider as required by applicable medical record-keeping laws. These are your clinic's legal records and exist independently of the app.

Retention period: deletion is completed within 30 days of verifying your request. Limited records of the deletion request itself may be kept for legal compliance.

Deleting some data without deleting your account

You can delete individual items (such as logged vitals, symptoms, meal entries, or uploaded documents) directly inside the app at any time. You may also email rsaini@ginihealth.in to request deletion of specific data while keeping your account active.
