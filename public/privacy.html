Privacy Policy

Last updated: 6 June 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Gini Health ("we", "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the Gini Health mobile application and related services (together, the "Service").

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information: your name, phone number, date of birth, and password.
  • Health information you provide: medications, vital signs (such as blood pressure, blood sugar, and weight), lab reports, symptoms, meal logs, goals, appointments, and medical documents you upload or scan.
  • Health information from your care team: if you are a patient of a clinic or hospital that uses Gini Health, your treatment records (prescriptions, lab results, visit summaries) may be linked to your account by your care team.
  • Camera and photos: used only when you choose to scan or upload a medical document. We do not access your camera or photo library in the background.
  • Device and usage data: device type, app version, push-notification token, and anonymised usage analytics that help us improve the app.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide the Service: showing your medications, reminders, vitals, reports, and care plan.
  • To connect you with your care team and let them review the information you log.
  • To send you medication reminders, appointment reminders, and important service notifications.
  • To improve the app's reliability, safety, and features.

We do not sell your personal or health information, and we do not use it for third-party advertising.

3. Sharing of Information

  • Your care team: doctors, clinical staff, and care coordinators involved in your care can see relevant health information.
  • Family members you link: if multiple family members share a phone number, each linked profile is visible on that account.
  • Service providers: we use trusted infrastructure providers (such as secure cloud hosting and analytics) that process data on our behalf under appropriate safeguards.
  • Legal requirements: we may disclose information if required by law or to protect the safety of users.

4. Data Security

Your data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS) and stored on secure cloud infrastructure with access controls. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we work to protect your information using industry-standard safeguards.

5. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service and to meet legal and medical record-keeping obligations. When data is no longer required, it is deleted or anonymised.

6. Your Rights

  • Access and review the information stored in your account.
  • Correct inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data (see below).
  • Withdraw consent for optional features (such as notifications) at any time from your device settings.

7. Account & Data Deletion

To request deletion of your Gini Health account and associated personal data, email us at rsaini@ginihealth.in with the subject "Account Deletion Request" from your registered contact details, or contact your clinic's front desk.

We will verify your identity and process the request within 30 days. Some records may be retained where required by law (for example, medical records that your hospital must keep under applicable regulations).

8. Children's Privacy

The Service is intended for use by adults. Profiles for minors may only be created and managed by a parent or guardian as part of family-linked care.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated version on this page with a revised "Last updated" date, and notify you in the app for material changes.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, contact us at rsaini@ginihealth.in.
